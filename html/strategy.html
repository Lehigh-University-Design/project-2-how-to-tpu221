How to play Go-Betwixt!

Strategy Video Progression
Kick Off
  • Because a player is eliminated when their last token rolls off the table, it’s sometimes strategic to use the edge and keep your aim low for better roll control.
  • If you successfully hit a ball off the table and your last stays on, that next person in the progression system advances you.
  • The act of knocking someone off the table counts as a successful turn.
Intentional Miss
  • There may be cases where intentionally missing a shot can be beneficial.
  • If the person after you is the progression lead, a risky hard elimination shot could place them closer to failure. A safe miss can sometimes be the smarter move.
  • Think two steps ahead—if the person playing after you has a very hard shot, you can eliminate them indirectly.
